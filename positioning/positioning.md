# Positioning and ICP

## The wedge

**DTC supplement and wellness brands doing £1–10m on Shopify, with stock split
across two or more regions (UK/EU/US) and reordering still done by feel.**

Multi-region is the qualifier, not just the niche. A single-warehouse brand
can run on a spreadsheet. The moment stock splits across warehouses, every
off-the-shelf tool gets awkward and someone starts reconciling by hand. That's
acute pain, and acute pain buys audits. It's also the least copyable part of
Joshua's background: plenty of people can set a reorder point for one
warehouse; few have run it across three regions with different lead times,
MOQs, and 3PLs. Supplements sharpen it further: expiry dates, batch tracking,
and repeat-purchase revenue that punishes stock-outs harder than a one-off
product.

## One-line positioning

Site header / LinkedIn bio:

> I build the ops backbone for supplement brands selling across UK, EU and
> US: one accurate view of stock in every warehouse, reorder points that fire
> before you run out, and the spreadsheet work automated away.

Cold email / verbal:

> I help supplement brands with stock in more than one warehouse stop
> running out, stop over-ordering, and stop reconciling spreadsheets by hand.

## ICP

| Dimension | Target | Signal |
|---|---|---|
| Category | Supplements, vitamins, wellness consumables | Repeat-purchase, expiry-dated stock |
| Revenue | £1–10m/yr | 8–40 staff, founder still close to ops |
| Platform | Shopify (incl. Plus) | — |
| Fulfilment | 2+ regions or warehouses/3PLs | Ships UK+EU or UK+US, "our US 3PL" |
| Tooling | Spreadsheets + inbox, or a half-used app | No ops hire, or one overloaded ops person |
| Pain | Stock-outs on hero SKUs, over-ordering, month-end reconciliation | Sold-out badges, restock waitlists |
| Buyer | Founder or Head of Ops | Person who does the 10pm spreadsheet session |

## Disqualifiers

- Single warehouse, no expansion planned — pain isn't acute enough.
- Under £1m (can't fund the build) or over £10m (needs an ERP and an ops
  team, not me).
- Already on a proper ERP (NetSuite, Unleashed used well) — problem's
  solved or political.
- Wants a custom app, a warehouse, or fulfilment renegotiation.
- Marketplace-first (Amazon-only) — different stock model.

## Credibility line

> I built this in-house at a supplements brand shipping UK, EU and US:
> Airtable as the single source of truth, inventory sync across 3PLs,
> reorder logic, KPI dashboards. It cut manual ops work by around 40% and
> took hero-SKU stock-outs to zero.

If you can tie either number to something harder (hours before/after, an
actual stock-out count) before you're on calls, do — a defensible number
beats a rounder one.
