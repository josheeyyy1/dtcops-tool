# The Three Offers

Audit → build → retainer. Each de-risks the next. The audit is priced to be
an easy yes; its report is the sales document for the build.

Prices below are applied throughout this repo (site, report template, call
script). Change them here and in `site/index.html` together if you land on
different numbers — nothing else depends on the exact figures.

---

## Offer 1: The Stock Audit — £2,000

A fixed-scope, two-week diagnostic of how stock moves from supplier PO to
customer door, across every warehouse.

**In:** 60-minute scoping call, read-only review of Shopify/3PL data and the
last 2 quarters of reorder history, a scored assessment across 8 dimensions,
a written report with a prioritised fix list and what each problem is
costing them, a 60-minute walkthrough call.

**Out:** No implementation, no tool changes, no write-access credentials.
Diagnosis, not surgery.

**Timeline:** 2 weeks, scoping call to walkthrough.

**Credited in full against the build if they proceed within 30 days.**

---

## Offer 2: The Ops Stack Build — £9,500

The same system built in-house, deployed for them.

**In:** Airtable base as single source of truth (products, suppliers, POs,
inventory by location, reorder rules), inventory sync from Shopify + 3PLs,
per-SKU per-region reorder logic with low-stock flags and suggested order
quantities, automated alerts (low stock / late PO / SLA breach), the KPI
dashboard, documentation + 90-minute handover, 30 days of post-launch
support.

**Out:** Custom app dev, ERP migration, 3PL renegotiation, finance systems,
demand-side work (paid media, CRO). Changes after 30 days are the retainer.

**Timeline:** 4–6 weeks, fixed scope agreed in writing before start.

**Payment:** 50% on start, 50% on handover. Minus the audit fee if within
30 days.

---

## Offer 3: Ops Partner — £1,500/month

Monthly ownership so the system keeps matching the business as it changes.

**In (per month):** monitoring and maintenance of syncs/alerts/dashboards,
up to 2 working days of changes (new SKUs, new automations, rule tuning),
a monthly 45-minute review against the dashboard, next-business-day
priority response.

**Out:** New-region/3PL integrations beyond the day allowance (quoted
separately), anything in the build's exclusion list, being their ops
employee.

**Timeline:** Monthly, 3-month minimum, then rolling with 30 days' notice.

---

## Pricing reasoning

| Offer | Floor | Applied | Stretch | Why |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Audit | £1,500 | **£2,000** | £2,500 | Below £1,500 attracts tyre-kickers. £2,000 is signable without a board conversation, and credited-against-build makes it near-free. |
| Build | £7,500 | **£9,500** | £14,000 (3+ regions) | One ops hire costs £35–45k/yr and doesn't build systems. If the audit shows £20k+/yr leaking, £9,500 with a 4–6 week payback is an easy case. |
| Retainer | £950/mo | **£1,500/mo** | £2,500/mo | ~2 days at a sane day rate, 10x cheaper than a hire. 3-month minimum protects against churn before the system proves itself. |

Once live: if 3 of your first 5 audit prospects say yes without flinching,
the price is too low — raise the next one.
